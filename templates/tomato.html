TOMATO

      


SCIENTIFIC NAME : Solanum lycopersicum

COMMON NAME : Tomato

ORIGIN : South America

SCIENTIFIC CLASSIFICATION:
  • Kingdom: Plantae
  • Division: Angiosperms
  • Order: Solanales
  • Family: Solanaceae
  • Genus: Solanum
CULTIVATION TIPS:
Sunlight :
1. Tomatoes thrive in full sunlight.
2. They should receive at least 6-8 hours of direct sunlight each day.
3. They can tolerate partial shade, but full sunlight promotes optimal growth.
Watering :
1. Young tomato plants require regular watering to establish roots.
2. Mature plants are drought-tolerant but benefit from occasional deep watering.
3. Ensure the soil is well-draining to prevent waterlogging, which can harm the roots.
Temperature :
1. Tomatoes prefer warm temperatures.
2. They grow best in temperatures between 65째F (18째C) and 85째F (29째C).
3. They are sensitive to frost and should be protected in cooler climates.
Soil Type:
1. Tomatoes prefer well-draining soil rich in organic matter.
2. They thrive in slightly acidic to neutral pH soil (6.0-7.0).
3. Soil should be enriched with compost or aged manure for optimal growth.
4. Avoid compacted or waterlogged soil to prevent root rot.

USES:
  • Culinary: Used in a wide variety of dishes, including salads, sauces, and soups.
  • Medicinal: Contains beneficial nutrients like vitamin C and antioxidants.
  • Ornamental: Some tomato varieties are grown for their attractive foliage and fruits in gardens.
  • Commercial: Grown on a large scale for commercial production of processed products like ketchup and tomato paste.
  • Home Gardening: Popular choice for home gardeners due to ease of cultivation and versatility in cooking.
